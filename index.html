Star To A Young Culture

Get Up Close and Personal with STAYC: Member Profiles and Their Journey

STAYC is a six-member girl group under High Up Entertaiment. They debuted on November 12, 2020, with SO BAD from the 1st single album Star To A Young Culture.

STAYC's name, "Star To A Young Culture," signifies their ambition to lead in the 'Teen-Fresh' genre and be the "Future of K-pop," captivating fans with youthful energy.
